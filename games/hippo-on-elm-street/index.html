Hippo on Elm Street

A not-so-scary story.

Baby, it's cold outside. All you want to do is snuggle up in a warm sock and take a nap. But something seems to be going on in the house, and you're determined to find out what it is.

This game is based in the world of the House Hippo, a PSA by the Concerned Children's Advertisers.
