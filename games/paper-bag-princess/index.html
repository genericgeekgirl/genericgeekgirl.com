The Paper Bag Princess

An interactive fairy tale.

Princess Elizabeth is about to marry the love of her life when a dragon attacks the castle and kidnaps her betrothed.

This game is loosely based upon the book of the same name by Robert Munsch. Permission to write the game was requested from and granted by Mister Munsch himself.

The artwork of Elizabeth was graciously provided by Stacey, aka “iduck” on deviantART.

Released September 2013.
