And a Hippo New Year

An interactive holiday tale.

'Twas the night before Christmas when all through the house, the only creature stirring was a hippo the size of a mouse.

This game was created for Yuletide 2012, an annual fanfiction exchange. It is based in the world of the House Hippo, a PSA by the Concerned Children's Advertisers.

Released December 2012.
