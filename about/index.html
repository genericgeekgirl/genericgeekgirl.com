Adri is a game developer and narrative designer from Boston, specializing in short, adorable games. In addition to creating games, she co-organizes the People's Republic of Interactive Fiction and Women in Games Boston, and is a director of digital curation for the Boston Festival of Indie Games. She has spoken at AlterConf, Arisia, LadiesCon and the Game Developer's Conference [video]. Three words that one might use to describe Adri are "generic", "geek" and "girl". Sometimes all at once.

Adri's work can be supported on Patreon or at itch.io. Source code for her games and other small projects is hosted on GitHub. She can be found talking about games, software, and whatever else interests her at the moment on Twitter.
